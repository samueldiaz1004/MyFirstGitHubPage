My Favorite Albums

A small list of my favorite albums in no particular order and limited to one album per band.

Pink Floyd - Wish You Were Here (1975)

Probably my favorite rock album of all time and the first vinyl I ever bought. Long songs with complex structures and beautiful composition.

Favorite Tracks:
  • Shine On You Crazy Diamond (Pts. 1-5)
  • Welcome to the Machine
  • Have a Cigar

Radiohead - OK Computer (1997)

One of the few alt rock bands I really love. This album is the perfect blend of acoustic and electronic elements.

Favorite Tracks:
  • Paranoid Android
  • Let Down
  • No Surprises

Black Sabbath - Master of Reality (1971)

The band that introduced me to the world of heavy metal. Surprisingly heavy and moody for its time.

Favorite Tracks:
  • Sweer Leaf
  • Children of the Grave
  • Into the Void

Megadeth - Peace Sells...But Who's Buying (1986)

I'm not sure which is my favorite thrash album or band but I come back to this album the most. It is technical, fast and unhinged.

Favorite Tracks:
  • Wake Up Dead
  • The Conjuring
  • Good Mourning / Black Friday

Death - Individual Thought Patterns (1993)

The band that hooked me into Extreme Music. Escential death metal band and album, both melodic and heavy.

Favorite Tracks:
  • Overactive Imagination
  • Trapped in a Corner
  • The Philosopher

Opeth - Ghost Reveries (2005)

This album comes from my favorite band of all time. It is brutal and lush. Complex but memorable. One of the greatest of all time.

Favorite Tracks:
  • Ghost of Perdition
  • Reverie / Harlequin Forest
  • Isolation Years

Square Enix - NieR Original Soundtrack (2010)

My favorite original soundtrack from one of my favorite games. This album has a great variety of songs ranging from delicate and intimate to epic in scale.

Favorite Tracks:
  • Grandma
  • Song of the Ancients / Devola
  • Shadowlord
